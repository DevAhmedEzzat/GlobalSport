Turf Technology

ENGINEERED FOR
PERFORMANCE

FIFA & World Rugby Certified

All systems are independently laboratory-tested and certified to the highest international governing body standards before installation begins.

UV-Stabilised Polyethylene Fibre

Premium monofilament and slit-film fibres engineered to resist UV degradation, colour fade, and mechanical wear over a decade of play.

Advanced Shock Absorption

EPDM rubber and crumb infill layering systems calibrated to HIC (Head Injury Criterion) and Gmax standards for player protection.

Integrated Permeability

Fully permeable backing systems achieving 1,000+ mm/hour drainage rates, eliminating waterlogging and ensuring 365-day playability.

Temperature Management

Reflective and cooling infill technologies that reduce surface temperatures by up to 15°C in direct sunlight compared to standard systems.

Recyclable & Sustainable

End-of-life recyclable turf systems and organic infill options available — we engineer with environmental responsibility built in from day one.
